Welcome to Consult Club, IIT Bombay!
Consulting is one of the most sought after preferences in regards to the placement scenario within the campus. The Consult Club has been founded with the aim to serve as a helping hand to the students who wish to pursue a career in consulting. Consult Club organizes workshops, interactive sessions, provides resources and is involved in a host of other activities for the same. However with time, we envision to broaden these horizons and to create a club driven by the passion for consulting.
