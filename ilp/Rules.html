Guidelines to Companies:

  • The Problem statement should be sufficiently well to be completed within a period of 45 days.
  • The problem should be majorly independent of visiting the industry.
  • If the expected expenditure on the problem is greater than 5000/-, the company is expected to do the needful.
  • The industries are requested to assign a mentor for each problem statement so that students can interact and get a better picture of the problem. The team size guidelines can be given by the company.
  • The mentors will be expected to keep a track of student progress along with one mid-term review of the project.
  • We are looking for some live industrial problems, which students will then be provided an opportunity to work on
  • Few company representatives are expected to visit the campus for ‘Industry Day’ to evaluate student performance.
  • It would be great if some incentive are provided from the company’s side in the form of mementos, gifts, prize money, goodies, internship offer etc.
  • IPR issues, if any, can be conveyed in advance by the company before launching the project or else, IRCC guidelines will be followed.
