Counselling Centre Students Support Services

The Counselling Care Centre, IIT Bombay has a TLC approach which includes Training (and sensitisation), Love (concern for all individuals) and Cooperation (from all student groups). Individual Confidential Counselling is available at Counselling Care Centre, 3rd Floor Main Building, IIT Bombay, Powai, Mumbai, India 400076.

The iCare IITB Facebook Page, launched on July 23, 2014, is an outreach initiative of the Counselling Care Centre IIT Bombay. It is a means to make counselling friendly and approachable to all students on campus.Students can use the Facebook Page to get regular tips on managing stress, anger, anxiety etc. Students can also come to know about any upcoming workshops organised by the Counselling Care Centre IIT Bombay such as academic stress management, EQ 101 etc.

Ms Shivani Manchanda, Counselling Coordinator, Counselling Care Centre, IIT Bombay
